The Downbeat Collective

Vocal improv community in the Bay Area

Our Mission

We seek to spread the joy of collaborative vocal improvisation and connect people through the healing power of making music together.

Sing with us

Everyone is welcome to come sing with us every Monday from 6-9pm at the Berkeley Alembic. Arrive 6-630pm for tea and greetings. Singing starts at 6:30pm and wraps by 8:30pm for clean-up and goodbyes until 9pm.
